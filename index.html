Product Definition

PopUpPlaydate is an app centralised around actual in-person meet ups as it allows parents and childminders of young children to arrange playdates locally, easily and quickly thus facilitating social interaction.

User Scenario

After giving 18 month old Bruno his breakfast and while drinking her coffee, Nina is planning the day ahead. She would like to have a playdate arranged for Bruno, but does not know any other parents with children of a similar age in the local area.

As it is a nice, sunny day today, Nina wants to spend it outside.

She takes her phone and opens the PopUpPlaydate application that she downloaded and registered with the evening before. She checks if anyone in Clongrin has created a playdate already, that she and Bruno can join in. She cannot find any, so she creates one herself, putting the local playground as the location and 11am as the scheduled time.

Afterwards she continues drinking her coffee and thinking what snacks she should take out with them.

After few minutes she hears the sound of incoming notification, which informs her that Mary O’Brien has just joined her playdate. She opens up Mary’s profile and learns that Mary is only 3 years older than Nina and has a girl, Siobhan, who is of a similar age to Bruno. Nina finishes her coffee and starts to dressing Bruno and pack the snacks for going out.

She is excited to meet a local Irish mum and is looking forward to Bruno meeting a new playmate.

Use case: Create PlayDateEvent

PlayDateEvent contain description, preferred languages, date-time, location, max numbers of participants and other properties

PRECONDITIONS:

PlayDateUser has an active account and a number of participants. Location service is enabled.

  • ● Set description of PlayDate Event
