PopUpPlaydates

Product Presentation Video

Product Definition

PopUpPlaydate is an app centralised around actual in-person meet ups as it allows parents and childminders of young children to arrange playdates locally, easily and quickly thus facilitating social interaction.

User Persona Profile

Nina Milic

“It doesn’t matter who I meet, whether they are Irish or not, a Mum or a Dad; as long as we get on well and the children have a fun time together, that’s what makes me happy.”

DETAILS:


Age: 32

Nationality: Croatian

Occupation: Primary School Teacher, now stay at home Mum

Level of Education: University

Mother Tongue: Croatian

Level of English: Fluent

Marital Status: Married to Marko, IT engineer, Croatian

Children: Bruno, aged 18 months

Location: Clongriffin, Co Dublin

BIO:

Nina was an English teacher in a Croatian primary school, but when her husband was offered a role in Ireland, she decided to leave her career for a better quality of family life in Ireland.

Marko has a good job and his salary is enough to support the family. Nina might be looking for a job when Bruno is 3 and qualifies under ECCE programme at a local kindergarten. She and Marko may have more children, in which case she will continue to stay at home. Nina comes from a big family herself and would love to have 2 or 3 children of her own.


PERSONALITY:

Shy | Eager


INTERESTS:

  • ● Reading
  • ● Watching movies online
  • ● Shopping online

GOALS:

  • ● Make new friends (preferably Irish)
  • ● Find playmates for Bruno
  • ● Expose Bruno to the English language daily

FRUSTRATIONS:

  • ● New to Ireland
  • ● Does not drive, relies on public transport which is difficult with a child in tow
  • ● Isolated
  • ● Does not have a lot in common with the parents at playgroup

TECHNOLOGY:

  • ● Internet: Competent user
  • ● WhatsApp: Keeps in touch with family and friends in Croatia
  • ● Facebook: Mums’ forum and personal use

BRANDS:

Zara | Gap | Nike | Highstreet labels

User Scenario

Nina is planning the day ahead after giving 18 month old Bruno his breakfast and while drinking her coffee. She would like to have a playdate arranged for Bruno but does not know any other parents with children of similar ages in her local area.

It is a nice sunny day today so Nina wants to spend it outside. She takes her phone and opens the PopUpPlaydate application that she downloaded and registered with the evening before. Nina checks if anyone in Clongriffin has created a playdate for her and Bruno to join. She cannot find any so she creates one herself, adding the local playground as the location and 11 am as the scheduled time.

Nina continues drinking her coffee and thinking about which snacks she should take out with them. After a few minutes she hears the sound of an incoming notification which informs her that Mary O’Brien has just joined her playdate. She opens up Mary’s profile and discovers that Mary is only 3 years older than Nina and has a girl Siobhan who is of a similar age to Bruno. Nina finishes her coffee and starts to dress Bruno then packs their snacks for going out.

Nina is excited to meet a local Irish mum and is looking forward to introducing Bruno to a new playmate.

Use Case Diagram

Use Case Specification

Use case: Create PlayDateEvent

PlayDateEvent contain description, preferred languages, date-time, location, max numbers of participants and other properties

PRECONDITIONS:

PlayDateUser has an active account. Location service is enabled.

SUCCESS GUARANTEE:

New PlayDateEvent will be uploaded on PlayDateServer and visible to other users.

MAIN SUCCESS SCENARIO:

  • ● Open template for a new PlayDate Event
  • ● Set description of PlayDate Event
  • ● Set location
  • ● Set date time
  • ● Set max number of participants
  • ● Publish PlayDateEvent
